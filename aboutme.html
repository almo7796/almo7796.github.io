About Me

These are some activities I enjoy doing in my free time.

Volunteering in my Community

I enjoy volunteering in my community, especially outdoors. Over the years I have volunteered at Habitat for Humanity, The Platt River Half Marathon, and other parks around the Denver Metro Area.

Working with People

I have worked many jobs in the customer service industry. I worked at McDonald's as my first job and loved the thrill of the fast paced environment and helping people. While at the University of Colorado I worked as a server at a restaurant in the beautiful Chautauqua national historic landmark in Boulder. I also was a community assisstant for my apartment complex and a tutor for the university's economic department.

Traveling

Travling is something that I am very passionate about. I have been lucky enough to travel to many places and experience many different cultures, and I plan to continue traveling for as long as possible!

Skiing

Being born and raised in Colorado has given me the opportunity to ski, and I came to love it! Nothing beats a weekend in the mountians with some friends!
